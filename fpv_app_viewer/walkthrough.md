## 🎨 Minimal Accordion Design System

The system follows clean, Notion-inspired design tokens:
- **Canvas Base:** Soft warm editorial background (`#FAF8F5`) paired with pure white container surfaces and soft gray-brown borders (`#E8E4DF`).
- **Typography:** Space Grotesk font pairings used for clear letter-spacing and hierarchy text labels.
- **Accents:** High-density terracotta orange (`#C2622D`) used to demarcate leaf bullet points.
